# Prompt Enhancer

Struggling with prompting? Not everyone is a prompt whisperer. Short, vague prompts lead to low quality memes. Our **Prompt Enhancer** ensures higher fidelity memes, every time, and on the fly.

No more trial-and-error or over-reliance on tools like ChatGPT. It’s made to work seamlessly with all Alphakek [meme models](meme-models.md).

## How It Works

***

Simply use `/image`, and our system will _instantly_ expand it into a professional-grade prompt that extracts maximum creativity and precision from our meme generator.

* Prompt Enhancer automatically works for prompts up to 350 characters
* Prompts longer than 350 characters will be opted out of the Prompt Enhancer
