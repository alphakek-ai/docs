## How to Upgrade

***

1. All new users will receive free Basic access automatically.
2. To upgrade User Tier, buy and hold $AIKEK.
   * [Uniswap](https://app.uniswap.org/#/swap?inputCurrency=ETH\&outputCurrency=0x8dce83eca4af45dbe618da1779f9aaca43201084)
   * [Tokenomics](../tokenomics.md)
3. Start the Alphakek Telegram bot [here](https://t.me/alphakek_bot).
4. Verify your $AIKEK holdings using the [/login command](bot-commands.md).

**Disclaimers**

* Using Alphakek services does not spend any of your $AIKEK tokens.
* The pricing model is in development and is subject to change.
